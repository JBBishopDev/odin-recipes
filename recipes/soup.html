Chicken Tortilla Soup

This is delicious soup. Soup is great for eating, and chicken tortilla soup in particular is tasty.

Here is the recipe.

  • Chicken
  • Soup Water
  • Spicey Stuff

Steps

  1. Cook and cut chicken
